1. Executive Financial Summary

* Total GMV: The total processed volume across all regions and categories stands at 115,621 USD.
* Top Performing Category: Electronics is the primary driver of volume, contributing 41,531 USD (approx. 36% of total revenue).
* Lowest Performing Category: Healthcare represents the smallest share with only 8,640 USD.

### 2. Regional & Payment Method Insights

* Dominant Region: APAC is the clear leader, accounting for 82,229 USD (71% of total global volume), significantly outperforming the EU and US combined.
* Preferred Payment Method: Card payments are the most popular globally (52,730 USD), followed closely by UPI (34,287 USD).
* Observation:UPI is highly concentrated in the APAC region, while Card payments show a more distributed usage across all three regions.
* Average Transaction Value (ATV): The "Home" category has the highest average transaction amount index at 131.71%, suggesting that while it may have fewer transactions than Electronics, each purchase is significantly higher in value.

### 3. Operational Health & Risk Analysis

* Status Distribution:
* Captured: 82,010 USD (Success Rate: ~71%).
* Failed (e05 timeout): 17,442.50 USD.
* Chargebacks: 16,168.50 USD.

* Risk Concern: The Grocery category is experiencing a disproportionately high amount of chargebacks (5,355 USD), which is higher than the chargebacks in the Electronics category despite Electronics having higher total volume.
* Merchant Breakdown (High-Risk/High-Value Filter):
* Alpha Mart and Beta Stores are the highest-activity merchants with 11 flagged transactions each.
* Out of the total flagged transactions, these two merchants account for 22 out of the 30 high-priority alerts (73% of all flags).



### 4. Key Recommendations
1. Investigate Grocery Chargebacks: High chargeback rates in Grocery suggest potential fraud or service fulfillment issues that need immediate review.
2. Optimize Timeout Issues: 17,442 USD is lost to "e05 timeout" errors. Technical investigation into the gateway latency is required to recover this lost revenue.
3. Merchant Monitoring: Focused account management is needed for Alpha Mart and Beta Stores due to their high frequency of flagged (High Risk/High Value) activity.
